Ingredients:
 - 1 can of Rotelle
 - 1 regular sized can of each of the following:  hominy or corn, black beans, red beans, kidney beans
 - 1 lb of chicken or ground beef
 - 1 packet of taco seasoning
 - 1 packet of ranch

Directions:
 1. Cook meat
 2. Put ingredients into large pot
 3. Add water until ingredients are submersed
 4. Heat to simmer for 30+ minutes
